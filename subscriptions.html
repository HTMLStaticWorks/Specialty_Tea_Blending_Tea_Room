Frequently Asked Questions

How are the monthly teas selected?

Our master blenders and sommeliers work months in advance to curate selections based on seasonal harvests, ensuring you receive the freshest and most vibrant profiles available.

Can I pause or cancel my subscription?

Absolutely. You can pause, skip a month, or cancel your subscription at any time through your member dashboard with zero hidden fees or obligations.

Are the virtual sommelier sessions recorded?

Yes. If you miss the live interactive session, a high-quality recording will be available in your member portal the next day so you can taste along at your own pace.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship our luxury tea boxes worldwide. However, shipping times and priority delivery perks may vary depending on your specific region.
