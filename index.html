Heritage

The Origin Story

Our single-origin teas are sourced directly from high-altitude artisan farms. Each leaf carries the distinct terroir of its mountainside, carefully preserved from the misty peaks to your cup.

We collaborate with multi-generational estates that champion sustainable cultivation. By honoring the natural rhythm of the seasons, we ensure every harvest yields a complex, unforgettable flavor profile steeped in centuries of tea-making tradition.
