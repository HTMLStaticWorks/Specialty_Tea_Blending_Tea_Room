Our Story

A legacy of passion, precision, and the relentless pursuit of the perfect cup.

Heritage

Sourced from the Clouds

Our journey began high in the misty peaks of ancient tea gardens. We partner directly with generational farmers who harvest only the finest leaves, ensuring ethical practices and unparalleled quality.

These remote, high-altitude environments provide the perfect climate for tea bushes to grow slowly, developing deep, complex flavors that define our exclusive collections.

  • Beyond Fair Trade Standards
  • Preserving Ancient Terroirs
  • Hand-Picked with Precision
Craftsmanship

The Art of Blending

Our master blenders treat tea making as a high art form. By combining rare botanicals and exotic spices, we create symphonies of flavor that awaken the senses.

Every batch undergoes a rigorous tasting process to ensure the aromatic profile remains consistent, perfectly balancing boldness with subtlety. We take pride in our innovative techniques that respect classic traditions while introducing modern flair.

Our Journey

A Legacy in the Making

From our humble beginnings to a global standard of tea luxury.

1995

The First Harvest

Our founder discovered a forgotten mountain tea garden, harvesting the first leaves that would become our signature blend. The dedication to quality was born.

2008

The Art of Blending

We established our first artisan blending facility, allowing our master blenders to experiment with rare botanicals from around the world to craft exquisite flavors.

2026

Global Luxury

Opening our flagship boutique in Botanical City, setting a new global standard for the luxury tea experience, blending tradition with modern elegance.

Sanctuary

Our Flagship Lounge

Step into our five-star boutique tea room. Designed with minimalist luxury, it is a haven for those who seek mindfulness and community over a steaming cup.

Featuring dedicated tasting rooms, bespoke sensory experiences, and a serene atmosphere, our flagship lounge is the physical embodiment of the Elysia philosophy—a space where time slows down.
